# Submitting Support Requests

Once logged into Zendesk, you can submit new tickets or view existing ones.

## Portal Options

- **Submit a Request** — Create a new support ticket  
- **Check Your Existing Requests** — View or update tickets you’ve submitted  

---

## How to Submit a Request

### 1. Choose Your Product
Select **Company/Customer Name** from the product dropdown.

> No need to select as you will automatically see only your company's requests.

---

### 2. Add a Subject
Provide a concise summary of the issue.  
Example:  
**“Links broken on Help and Support page.”**

---

### 3. Write a Detailed Description

Include all relevant information:

- Issue summary  
- Steps to reproduce  
- Environment (Prod, Staging, UAT, etc.)  
- User/role impacted  
- Acceptance criteria  
- Screenshots or video links  

> Please be sure to include all relevant information, which may include screenshots or video URL, steps to recreate issue, user/role impacted, Acceptance Criteria, etc.

---

### 4. Add CCs (Optional)

Add team members who should receive updates.  
No need to CC Level Data — they receive all submissions automatically.

---

### 5. Attach Files (Optional)

Upload screenshots, videos, or other supporting materials.

---

### 6. Submit the Ticket

Click **Submit** to send your request to the Support team.
