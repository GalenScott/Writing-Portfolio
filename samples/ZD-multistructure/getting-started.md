# Getting Started with Zendesk

Zendesk is Level Data’s platform for managing CEPI (MI School Data) support requests. It enables fast triage, coordinated communication, and escalation when needed.

>Zendesk is a customer service software that helps us manage customer support operations.

---

## 1. Access the Support Portal

Navigate to the Level Data Zendesk Support link provided during onboarding.

Bookmark the page for quick access.

---

## 2. Create Your Account

At the login screen, select **“New to Level Data Support? Sign up.”**

You will be prompted to:

- Enter your email address  
- Create a secure password  

If you receive an error, verify your email or reset your password.

---

## 3. Log In

Once registered, log in using your email and password.

If you need help accessing the portal, contact:

helpdesk@example.com
